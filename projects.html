Diabetes Risk Prediction

View Project Details

Tech Stack: Python, Pandas, Numpy, Scikit-learn

  • Objective: Developed a predictive model to identify high-risk diabetic patients for early intervention.
  • Data Preparation: Processed over 253,000 BRFSS records, focusing on data cleaning, balancing using class weight, and scaling.
  • Modeling: Implemented Logistic Regression, Random Forest, and XGBoost models with feature selection techniques (correlation matrix, Chi-square test, variance inflation factor) and cross-validation.
  • Outcome: Chose Logistic Regression (71.26% accuracy, 74.28% recall) over XGBoost (85.76% accuracy, 11.72% recall) to prioritize recall, reducing false negatives and enhancing the detection of at-risk patients.

Impact of COVID19 on Hygiene Habits

View Project Details

Tech Stack: Python, Statistical Analysis & Hypothesis testing

  • Objective: Investigated the shifts in consumer hygiene behaviors and spending patterns due to the COVID19 pandemic.
  • Data Preparation: Processed 122 survey responses, analyzing demographic factors, hygiene practices, and changes in spending.
  • Analysis: Employed hypothesis testing, MANOVA, and Chi-squared tests to uncover significant behavioral differences across age groups and demographics.
  • Outcome: Identified a significant increase in hygiene awareness and product usage post-pandemic, with variations influenced by age and employment status.
